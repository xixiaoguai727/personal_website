About Me

Hi! My name is Xi Zheng, and I am a Ph.D. candidate in the Department of Computer Science at the BiWell Lab, City University of Hong Kong, supervised by Dr. Yuhan Luo .

My research interests include human–AI interaction for health and wellbeing, and multimodal human–AI interaction.

Before starting my Ph.D. in HCI at CityU, I was trained in mathematics and statistics. I received my undergraduate degree, majoring in Mathematics and Statistics, from the University of Toronto, and my master’s degree in Statistics from the National University of Singapore.
